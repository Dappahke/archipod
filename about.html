About The ArchiPod

The ArchiPod is your go-to platform for insightful discussions on architecture, sustainability, and urban design. We bring together experts, students, and enthusiasts to explore groundbreaking architectural projects, trends, and innovations shaping our built environment.

Our Mission

We strive to bridge the gap between architectural theory and practice by featuring industry leaders, discussing sustainable solutions, and providing in-depth analysis of architectural competitions and projects worldwide.

What We Cover

- Exclusive interviews with renowned architects and firms.
- Reviews of architectural competitions in Africa and beyond.
- Case studies of iconic structures by Zaha Hadid, Bjarke Ingels, and other greats.
- Sustainable architecture and emerging technologies.
- Urban planning and real estate developments.
