About The ArchiPod

Exploring architecture, innovation, and sustainability—one episode at a time.

Meet Our Founder

Noel

Noel is a passionate architect and design enthusiast who founded The ArchiPod to bring conversations about architecture, innovation, and sustainability to a global audience. With a vision to bridge the gap between traditional architecture and future-forward design, Noel curates insightful discussions with industry leaders, emerging architects, and design thinkers.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To create a platform where architects, designers, and enthusiasts can engage in thought-provoking discussions about the evolving world of architecture.

Vision: To inspire a new generation of architects to design with innovation, sustainability, and purpose.
